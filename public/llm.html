Stoke Plumbers and Boilers

Professional plumbing and boiler company serving Stoke-on-Trent and Staffordshire.

Company Overview

Stoke Plumbers and Boilers provides professional plumbing, boiler, and drainage services to residential and commercial customers across Stoke-on-Trent and the surrounding Staffordshire area. We operate 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, 365 days a year.

Our services include boiler repairs and servicing, drain unblocking, CCTV drain inspections, high-pressure jetting, and emergency plumbing response. We use professional-grade equipment, employ Gas Safe registered engineers, and offer no call-out fees with fixed pricing.

Services Offered

  • Boiler Repairs and Servicing – Gas Safe registered engineers for boiler breakdowns, annual servicing, and full system diagnostics
  • Blocked Drains – Clearing blockages in sinks, toilets, baths, showers, and external drains
  • CCTV Drain Surveys – HD camera inspections providing detailed video reports for diagnosis, insurance, or pre-purchase surveys
  • Drain Jetting – High-pressure water jetting for domestic and commercial properties to clear stubborn blockages
  • Emergency Plumbing – 24/7 emergency call-outs for burst pipes, boiler failures, flooding, and urgent plumbing problems
