Blocked Drains Manchester

Professional drainage company serving Manchester and Greater Manchester.

Company Overview

Blocked Drains Manchester provides professional emergency and scheduled drain services to residential and commercial customers across Manchester and the surrounding Greater Manchester area. We operate 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, 365 days a year.

Our services include drain unblocking, CCTV drain inspections, high-pressure jetting, and emergency response for flooding and sewage issues. We use professional-grade equipment and offer no call-out fees with fixed pricing.

Services Offered

  • Blocked Drains – Clearing blockages in sinks, toilets, baths, showers, and external drains
  • Drain Unblocking – Professional unblocking of internal and external drains using specialist equipment
  • CCTV Drain Surveys – HD camera inspections providing detailed video reports for diagnosis, insurance, or pre-purchase surveys
  • Drain Jetting – High-pressure water jetting for domestic and commercial properties to clear stubborn blockages
  • Emergency Drain Services – 24/7 emergency call-outs for flooding, sewage backups, and urgent drainage problems
